# Quantitative & Logical Aptitude Study Guide

This guide covers the essential topics and preparation strategies for the aptitude section of your job test.

---

### 1. Key Topics

#### A. Quantitative Aptitude
This section tests your mathematical skills and problem-solving ability under time pressure.

- **Core Arithmetic:**
  - Percentages
  - Profit & Loss
  - Simple & Compound Interest
  - Averages
  - Ratios & Proportions
- **Time, Speed, and Work:**
  - Time, Speed & Distance
  - Time & Work
  - Pipes & Cisterns
- **Other Important Topics:**
  - Permutations & Combinations
  - Probability
  - Number Systems

#### B. Logical Reasoning
This section assesses your ability to think logically and derive conclusions from given information.

- **Arrangements & Relations:**
  - Seating Arrangements (Linear, Circular)
  - Puzzles
  - Blood Relations
- **Deductive Reasoning:**
  - Syllogisms
  - Coding & Decoding
  - Number & Letter Series
  - Direction Sense
- **Data Interpretation:**
  - Reading and interpreting data from charts, graphs, and tables.

#### C. Verbal Ability
This section evaluates your command of the English language.

- **Comprehension:**
  - Reading Comprehension passages
- **Vocabulary:**
  - Synonyms & Antonyms
  - Idioms & Phrases
- **Grammar:**
  - Sentence Correction
  - Error Spotting

---

### 2. How to Prepare

- **Practice Daily:** Consistency is more important than intensity. Dedicate at least 30-60 minutes every day to solving aptitude questions.
- **Focus on Shortcuts:** For the Quantitative section, learn shortcut formulas and mental math tricks to save time. For Reasoning, learn to visualize arrangements and use diagrams.
- **Timed Drills:** Don't just solve problems; solve them against the clock. Time yourself for each section to improve your speed.
- **Analyze Mistakes:** After a practice session, review every mistake. Understand why you got it wrong and how to correctly approach it next time.

- **Online Platforms:**
